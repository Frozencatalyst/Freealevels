About

This website is dedicated to providing completely free, easy-to-use learning resources for A level Science and Maths in order to help students maximize their learning potential irrespective of the quality of their school education, and or socio-economic standing. The project was initially designed and developed (and currently maintained) by Freddie Gray, 2018.

Where to start?

To make the most of this website, we recommend using the textbook in conjunction with other resources (such as officially endorsed materials) and making use of Revision resources when preparing for tests or the final exams. Although the textbooks can be read in chronological order it is not recommended; instead dip-in-and-out of topics you are currently studying at school or are reviewing. Follow along with a pencil and paper at the ready to jot down notes and answer questions.

Volunteer

We would really appreciate the support and help of fellow teachers and students in respective subjects to either help correct and review created material or contribute new material such as videos and question packs. Also we would be especially appreciative of help in writing Maths and Further Maths notes for other modules, and for all subjects help in covering content for all exam boards.

If you would be interested in volunteering please email: FreeAlevels@gmail.com. Whilst we cannot offer financial benefits, we will of course give you attribution to contributions on the website and advertise/link to any internet projects you are involved in.
